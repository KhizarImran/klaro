# What Is a Quantfund?

A **quantitative fund** ("quantfund") trades systematically. Instead of a human
deciding each entry by feel, a set of rules — expressed as code — decides what to
buy, when, and how much. Your job as the builder is to turn a market *edge* into
software that runs without you.

By the end of this course you'll have built exactly that, in Python, trading FX
through MetaTrader 5.

## The four jobs of a quantfund

Every systematic strategy, no matter how fancy, does four things:

1. **Get data** — prices, in a clean, trustworthy form.
2. **Generate signals** — decide long / short / flat from that data.
3. **Size and manage risk** — decide *how much* and cap the downside.
4. **Execute** — place and manage real orders at the broker.

Wrap those in something that runs 24/5 and reports its results, and you have a fund.

## Why MT5 + FX

- **MT5** exposes a first-class Python API (`MetaTrader5`) — data, orders, account
  state, all from Python.
- **FX** runs nearly 24 hours, has deep liquidity, and demo accounts are free — so
  you can build and test the whole pipeline at zero risk before a cent is live.
